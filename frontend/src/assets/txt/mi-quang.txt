Ingredients for Quang Noodles:
300g chicken breast
200g pork bones
150g pork belly
200g shrimp
1 chicken egg (or 8 quail eggs)
1 kg fresh Quang noodles
1-2 pieces of rice paper (bánh tráng nướng)
2 tablespoons annatto oil
10g turmeric powder
5g chili powder
2 garlic cloves
1 shallot
Roasted peanuts
Mixed herbs: lettuce, mint, bean sprouts, etc.
Common seasonings: salt, pepper, sugar, fish sauce, oyster sauce

Instructions for Quang Noodles:
1. Prepare Ingredients: Peel and finely chop the garlic and shallot. Peel the onion and cut into wedges.

2. Prepare the Chicken: Separate the chicken meat from the bone; set the meat aside, and use the bones for the broth.

3. Make the Broth: Clean the pork and chicken bones, blanch them briefly in boiling water, then rinse with cold water. Place the bones in a pot with water, onion, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1 tablespoon seasoning powder, and simmer for 30 minutes to create the broth.

4. Season the Shrimp and Prepare the Eggs: Peel and devein the shrimp, then season with 1 teaspoon seasoning powder and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Boil the egg, peel, and cut into pieces.

5. Marinate the Pork Belly and Chicken: Clean and cut the pork belly and chicken into bite-sized pieces. Marinate with minced garlic and shallot, 2 tablespoons fish sauce, 1.5 tablespoons seasoning powder, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1/2 tablespoon oyster sauce, 2 teaspoons turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon chili powder, and 1 teaspoon pepper. Let sit for 15 minutes.

6. Final Steps: Heat a pan, add 2 tablespoons annatto oil, and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant. Add the marinated meat and cook until done. Pour the broth into the pan with the meat, then add the eggs and shrimp. Simmer for another 20 minutes, then turn off the heat. To serve, place noodles in a bowl, add meat, shrimp, and egg pieces, sprinkle with roasted peanuts, and pour in a bit of the sauce and broth. Serve with rice paper, peanuts, scallions, and fresh herbs.
