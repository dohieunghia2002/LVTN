Ingredients for Long Xuyen Broken Rice:
500g broken rice
300g pork (choose from ribs, loin, or belly)
30ml coconut milk
10ml lime juice
30g pandan leaves
5 duck eggs
2g five-spice powder
5g minced garlic
5g minced chili
10 shallots
Chopped green onions
10 pickled vegetables (to taste)
Pork skin (bì)
3-4 star anise
Seasonings: oyster sauce, cooking oil, salt, fish sauce, soy sauce, monosodium glutamate (MSG), granulated sugar

Instructions for Long Xuyen Broken Rice:
1. Prepare and Cook the Broken Rice: Rinse the pork with salted water, drain, and slice into thin pieces. Boil the duck eggs, then peel. Peel and finely chop the shallots. Rinse the broken rice, soak for 15 minutes, then drain and place in a bowl. Set up a steamer and add the rice to the steamer basket, seasoning with ½ teaspoon of salt and 1 tablespoon of cooking oil. Add some chopped pandan leaves, cover, and steam for 30 minutes until cooked, then fluff the rice.

2. Marinate and Grill the Pork: In a bowl, mix 60g honey, 30ml fish sauce, 30ml coconut milk, 30g oyster sauce, 10ml cooking oil, and 2g five-spice powder. Add the pork slices and marinate for 30 minutes. After marinating, skewer the meat and grill over charcoal for about 20 minutes.

3. Braised Duck Eggs: In a pot, melt sugar until it turns a caramel color, then add 10g minced shallots and sauté until fragrant. Pour in 400ml coconut water, 20ml soy sauce, 20ml fish sauce, 20g oyster sauce, and 3-4 star anise. Stir well and bring to a boil, then gently add the duck eggs and braise for 30-40 minutes until the eggs take on a beautiful caramel color.

4. Prepare Scallion Oil and Dipping Sauce: Heat 20ml cooking oil until hot, then pour it over chopped green onions mixed with ½ teaspoon salt and ½ teaspoon MSG. In a separate bowl, mash 3 cloves of garlic and 2 chili peppers. Add 2 tablespoons of sugar, 10ml lime juice, and 2 tablespoons of fish sauce to the garlic mixture and stir until well combined.

5. Serve: Place the broken rice on a plate, slice the grilled pork and braised duck eggs into bite-sized pieces, and arrange them around the rice. Drizzle some scallion oil over the rice, add pickled vegetables, some pork skin, and serve with the sweet and sour dipping sauce. Enjoy your delicious Long Xuyen broken rice dish!
