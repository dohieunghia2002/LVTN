Ingredients:
Roasted pork
Rice vermicelli (bánh hỏi)
Green onions, fried shallots
Sweet and sour fish sauce
Fresh vegetables: 1 carrot, 1 cucumber, lettuce, cherry tomatoes

Instructions:

1. Buy rice vermicelli (bánh hỏi) from the supermarket, soak it in cold water for 1-2 minutes to loosen it, then steam for 5 minutes.
2. Save the pork drippings to fry the green onions for a rich scallion oil. Season with 1/2 teaspoon of seasoning powder for extra flavor.
3. Slice the carrot and cucumber into bite-sized pieces, arrange them on a plate with lettuce and cherry tomatoes. Wrap them with the pork slices for an absolutely delightful meal!
